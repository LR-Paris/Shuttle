Q: How will I be billed for my order?
A: LR Paris will facilitate billing through the Michael Kors Global Brand Marketing team. Invoices will be shared with MK HQ and billed back to individual regions/teams.

Q: How much does shipping cost?
A: Shipping is calculated based on your location and the items in your order. If choosing to ship with LR Paris Freight Forwarder, Global Brand Marketing will confirm shipping costs associated with your order after the portal is closed. Local region to approve final shipping cost prior to production. Shipping + taxes to be included in final invoice.

If regions use their own Freight Forwarder, shipping and taxes to be handled directly with your third party.

Q: What is the production lead time?
A: Lead-time of mass production is 50 days after receipt of the order.

Q: What are the estimated transit times?
A: Transit times are estimated. Please verify with the logistics team for exact timelines.
Air: approximately 12-15 days.
Sea: approximately 35-45 days.

Q: Where are the items manufactured?
A: All items are made in China.

Q: How can I order less than one carton?
A: We cannot support orders smaller than one carton. All items are shipped in full carton quantities only.
